Chrome Dino Game

The Chrome Dino Game was released in 2014 by Google. It appears when users lose internet connection in Chrome.

Players control a dinosaur that jumps over obstacles and avoids enemies. It's simple, fun, and addictive.

Untitled Goose Game

Untitled Goose Game is a stealth puzzle game developed by House House. Players take the role of a mischievous goose in a quaint English village.

Complete a to-do list of tasks that mostly involve disrupting the lives of the unsuspecting villagers. It's delightfully chaotic.

Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird is a mobile game developed by Vietnamese programmer Dong Nguyen and released in 2013. It became a viral sensation worldwide.

Players tap the screen to keep the bird airborne while navigating through a series of pipes. It's brutally difficult but impossible to put down.
